About Alliance

Aquaculture Science, Delivered to Your Pond

Alliance is a Hyderabad-based aquaculture solutions company dedicated to profitable and sustainable fish & shrimp farming. We combine microbiology, nutrition science, and hands-on field knowledge to build products that genuinely work in Indian pond conditions.

Every formulation — from our potent multi-strain probiotics to chelated mineral supplements — is designed to solve real problems: controlling toxic buildup, boosting immunity, optimizing growth, and protecting the pond ecosystem.
